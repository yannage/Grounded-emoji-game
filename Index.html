How to Play

Goal

Watch as many TV shows as possible without getting caught by your parents!

Controls

  • Click on a floor tile to move
  • Click the TV button when next to a TV to watch
  • Click the Hide button when next to a trash bin to hide
  • Click the Lock Door button when next to a door to lock it
  • Click End Turn to advance time by 30 minutes

Tips

  • The TV must be on during a show's scheduled time to earn points
  • Parents get suspicious when they hear the TV
  • Lock doors to keep parents out of rooms
  • Hide in trash bins when parents are approaching
  • Spend points on upgrades for better abilities!
